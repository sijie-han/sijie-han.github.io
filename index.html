Research

Themes I keep coming back to.

Simulation that matters

I’m interested in simulation environments that are not just visually realistic, but also behaviorally realistic: interaction physics, task structure, and evaluation that predicts real-world performance.

Procedural benchmarks

Large-scale procedural generation can create the distribution shift we need to test robustness. I like tools that make it easy to sweep difficulty, scene composition, and sensor noise.

Prompt-to-3D systems

I’m exploring how vision-language models can convert natural language into structured, editable environments — bringing “prompting” into the loop for embodied research and evaluation.
